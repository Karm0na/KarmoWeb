[About]

Hello! I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Malaga and I combine code, hardware, and radical thinking in a tireless routine. My days are divided between developing retro-style horror games, lifting weights at the gym, and delving into political philosophy from a critical and communist perspective. I've been training seriously for over two years, transforming my 120 kg body into a stronger version of myself, with the goal of achieving a defined body. I believe that discipline isn't optional: it's the price of freedom. When it comes to programming, I'm motivated by both creative expression and technical challenge. I specialize in game development and systems programming, with practical experience in C++, Java, C, ARM Assembly, HTML/CSS, and Unity (C#). I'm currently working on Hollow Seraph, a PSX-style horror game, and Lify, an Android app for track gym progress. My life is a work in progress, but the mission is clear: sharpen my mind, sculpt my body, maintain authenticity, and never give up.
